Treating Common Pet Illnesses & Minor Injuries

Our Ballantyne clinic treats conditions that require immediate attention but are not life-threatening.

Vomiting & Diarrhea

Stomach upset, dietary indiscretion, or sudden nausea.

Ear Infections

Shaking head, scratching ears, redness, or odor.

UTI & Urinary Issues

Straining to pee, accidents in house, or blood in urine.

Limping & Lameness

Sudden favor of a leg, paw pain, or minor soft tissue injury.

Eye Irritation

Redness, squinting, discharge, or cherry eye.

Skin & Allergies

Hot spots, hives, severe itching, or rashes.

Minor Wounds

Bite wounds, cuts, scrapes, or broken nails.

Toxin Ingestion

Chocolate, grapes, or minor household toxin exposure.

Common Questions

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes! We welcome walk-ins at all our urgent care locations. However, we recommend checking availability online or calling ahead to reduce your wait time.

How much does a visit cost vs. the ER?

Our urgent care exams are significantly more affordable than an Emergency Room visit. While an ER exam can cost $150-$300+, our urgent care exams start at $85.

What if my pet has a life-threatening emergency?

If your pet has collapsed, is having seizures, severe bleeding, or trouble breathing, please go directly to the nearest 24-hour Emergency Hospital. We are equipped for urgent, stable conditions.

Can I use my pet insurance?

Absolutely. We accept all major pet insurance providers (Nationwide, Trupanion, Lemonade, Healthy Paws, etc.) and will provide you with all the records needed to file your claim.

Do you treat cats and dogs only?

We primarily treat dogs and cats. For exotic pets (birds, reptiles, small mammals), please call us first to ensure we have a specialist available.

What are your hours of operation?

We are open 7 days a week, typically from 8am to 6pm, including weekends, to be there when you need us most.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we accept Debit, Credit, and offer flexible payment options through Scratch Pay and CareCredit.

Do you provide dental services?

Yes! We offer dental exams, cleanings, and treatments to keep your pet's oral health in top shape.

Do you perform surgeries?

Yes, our expert vets perform spays, neuters, mass removals, and other soft tissue surgeries in our state-of-the-art clinics.

Can I access my pet's records online?

Yes, our digital portal allows you to easily view your pet's medical records, visit history, and care instructions anytime.
